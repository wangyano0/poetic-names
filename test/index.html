A Glimpse of Poetic Names / 探寻诗意之名

清扬 Qīng Yáng

Meaning: Clear, pure, and spirited.

From 'The Book of Odes' (《诗经》)

“有美一人，清扬婉兮。”

"A beautiful lady, with clear eyes and a graceful brow."

嘉树 Jiā Shù

Meaning: A symbol of nobility and integrity.

From 'Songs of Chu' (《楚辞》)

“后皇嘉树，橘徕服兮。”

"Oh, you noble and beautiful orange tree, you are born to this soil."

望舒 Wàng Shū

Meaning: The mythical charioteer of the moon.

From 'Songs of Chu' (《楚辞》)

“前望舒使先驱兮，后飞廉使奔属。”

"I order Wang Shu, the moon's charioteer, to lead the way."
